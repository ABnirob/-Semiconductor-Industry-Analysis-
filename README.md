# 📖 Project Overview

The **Semiconductor Industry Analysis Dashboard** is an executive-level Business Intelligence project designed to evaluate the global semiconductor industry using the **Global Competitiveness Positioning System (GCPS)** framework.

This project analyzes the industry's **growth potential, profitability, competitive landscape, risk factors, and value chain**, helping business leaders, investors, and analysts understand the current state of one of the world's most strategic industries.

The dashboard follows a modern **Power BI executive reporting** design with interactive KPIs, financial metrics, competitive benchmarking, and strategic insights.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

This project aims to:

- Analyze industry growth and profitability.
- Evaluate industry risks and external challenges.
- Measure competition intensity and market concentration.
- Examine the semiconductor value chain.
- Identify strategic opportunities for investors and businesses.
- Support data-driven decision-making through executive dashboards.

# 📌 Business Problem

The semiconductor industry powers nearly every modern technology, including AI, cloud computing, autonomous vehicles, IoT, smartphones, and consumer electronics.

Organizations require comprehensive industry intelligence to answer questions such as:

- Is the semiconductor industry attractive for investment?
- What are the industry's biggest risks?
- Who are the leading competitors?
- How concentrated is the market?
- Which stages of the value chain create the most value?
- What trends will shape future growth?

# 📊 Industry Analysis

## Industry Potential, Growth & Profitability

The analysis highlights:

- Strong demand for AI chips and cloud infrastructure.
- Significant investments in semiconductor manufacturing.
- High profitability among leading companies.
- Increasing adoption of advanced semiconductor technologies.
- Government support through national semiconductor initiatives.

---

## Industry Risk

Key risks identified include:

- Supply chain disruptions
- Geopolitical tensions
- Rapid technological change
- High capital investment requirements
- Market cyclicality
- Regulatory uncertainty

---

## Competition & Market Concentration

Major industry players include:

- NVIDIA
- Intel
- AMD
- TSMC
- Samsung Electronics
- Qualcomm

Competition is driven by:

- Innovation
- Manufacturing efficiency
- R&D investment
- Product performance
- AI capabilities

---

## Value Chain Analysis

The semiconductor value chain consists of:

1. Research & Design
2. Wafer Fabrication
3. Assembly & Testing
4. Distribution
5. End-Market Applications

The analysis shows that chip design and advanced manufacturing create the highest value and profitability.

---

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- AI is the primary growth catalyst for the semiconductor industry.
- Cloud computing continues to drive long-term demand.
- High barriers to entry protect established industry leaders.
- Supply chain resilience is becoming a strategic priority.
- Innovation and R&D investment remain the most important competitive advantages.
- The value chain demonstrates strong specialization, enabling high-value creation across design and manufacturing stages.
